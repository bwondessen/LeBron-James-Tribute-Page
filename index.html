LeBron James

Philanthropist, businessman, athlete

  • 2003

    - In the 2003 NBA Draft, LeBron was selected number one overall to his hometown team, the Cleveland Cavaliers. In his first season, he became the franchise’s first player – and the youngest player ever – to win the NBA Rookie of the Year Award.

  • 2004

    - LeBron's mission to change the world began as he established The LeBron James Family Foundation.

  • 2011

    - LeBron re-focused the Foundation's efforts on the life-changing power of education and established the I PROMISE Program. Taking on a new class of Akron Public School 3rd graders each year...

  • 2015

    - In 2015, LeBron takes it a step further. He partnered with The University of Akron to guarantee all of his eligible I PROMISE students free, four year college scholarships. The announcement immediately changes the dreams and long-term plans of families across Akron.

  • 2016

    - LeBron led the Cavaliers back from a 3-1 deficit to win the franchise’s first championship and the city’s first title in more than 50 years. He was again voted Finals MVP. He would bring the Cavaliers back to the finals the following two seasons, capping an historic run of eight-straight finals appearances..

  • 2016

    - LeBron establishes the I PROMISE Institute on the University of Akron campus as an around-the-clock resource center for his I PROMISE students, acclimating them to life on a college campus.

  • 2017

    - The Ohio Board of Education formally approves the designs and creation of the I PROMISE School in Akron. Educators, civic leaders, academic experts, community members, parents and students immediately get to work on the creation of the curriculum, innovative school year, and wrap-around supports IPS will provide.

  • 2018

    - On July 30, 2018 the I PROMISE School opened its doors for an historic first day of school for its inaugural class of 240 I PROMISE students.

  • 2019

    - LeBron announces the I PROMISE Village by Graduate Hotels for students and their families who need a safe, comforting place to stay as they work through challenges they may be facing including homelessness, domestic violence, and other unforeseen circumstances.

  • 2020

    - Taking their shared commitment to LeBron’s kids to a new level, Kent State University offered the life-changing opportunity of free tuition to LeBron’s Akron I PROMISE Network students. Now the I PROMISE students have a world of opportunity and choices for their next educational step.

  • 2020

    - Inspired by the kids and families in Akron and the promises we’ve been making since 2011, LeBron’s first children’s book “I PROMISE” shares the program’s motivational and uplifting message with the masses.
